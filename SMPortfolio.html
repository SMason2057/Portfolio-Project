About Me

Hello there! I'm glad you've decided to stop by and visit my page. My name is Shauna Mason and I am a completely normal human being. In addition to being a human, I love video games, puzzles, coding, and most importantly cats (I like dogs as well, but not as much as cats). I graduated from Valencia College with an A.S. degree a couple of years ago and I am now currently attending the University of Central Florida and working towards my B.S. degree in Information Technology (IT). Besides being a student, I also work at my college for the Center of Distributed Learning (CDL). My main role is to ensure that the online content for courses is accessible for students who have disabilities. To find out more just skip on down to the resources section to take a look at my resume. Although I am majoring in IT, I have fallen into web development. I have dabbled in the world of web for a few of my courses at Valencia, but it never really caught my interest.
